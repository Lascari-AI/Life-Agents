# Self-Employment CPA Document Checklist

Use this to verify all necessary documents are collected before packaging.

## Required Documents

### Income
- [ ] All 1099-NEC / 1099-MISC forms received
- [ ] Income summary or P&L (monthly breakdown preferred)
- [ ] Business bank account statements (all 12 months)

### Expenses
- [ ] Business expense ledger (categorized by type)
- [ ] Receipts for large purchases (>$500)
- [ ] Vehicle mileage log (if applicable)

### Deductions
- [ ] Home office write-up (sq ft calculation)
- [ ] Health insurance premium documentation
- [ ] Internet/utilities bills (if claiming home office)
- [ ] Rent receipts or lease (if claiming home office)

### Tax Payments
- [ ] Federal quarterly estimated tax payment confirmations (Q1-Q4)
- [ ] State quarterly estimated tax payment confirmations (Q1-Q4)

### Retirement
- [ ] Roth IRA contribution records
- [ ] SEP IRA contribution records (if applicable)
- [ ] 401(k) contribution records (if applicable)

### Personal Info
- [ ] Current address (note changes mid-year)
- [ ] Filing status
- [ ] Dependents (if any)
- [ ] Bank account info for direct deposit of refund

### Other (If Applicable)
- [ ] 1099-INT (interest income from savings/investments)
- [ ] 1099-DIV (dividend income)
- [ ] 1099-B (stock sales)
- [ ] W-2 (if any W-2 employment during year)
- [ ] 1098 (mortgage interest, if applicable)
- [ ] Student loan interest (1098-E)
- [ ] Charitable donation receipts
- [ ] State/local tax payments beyond estimates
